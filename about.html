Our Story

A legacy of excellence in luxury automotive retail, built on passion, integrity, and unmatched customer service

Founded on Passion

LRS Trade Cars was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create the ultimate luxury car buying experience. Founded in 2010 by automotive enthusiasts with decades of industry experience, we recognized a gap in the market for a dealership that truly understood the needs of discerning luxury car buyers.

What started as a small showroom in Manhattan has grown into New York's premier destination for luxury vehicles, serving clients from across the tri-state area and beyond. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we're proud to offer one of the most comprehensive selections of luxury vehicles in the region, backed by a team of experts who share our passion for automotive excellence and customer satisfaction.
