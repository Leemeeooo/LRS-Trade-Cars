Frequently Asked Questions

What are your financing options?

+

We offer a wide range of financing options through our partnerships with leading financial institutions. Our rates are competitive, and we can tailor payment plans to fit your budget. Contact our finance department for a personalized quote.

Do you accept trade-ins?

+

Yes, we accept trade-ins and offer competitive market values. Our appraisal process is transparent and fair. You can get an instant estimate online or bring your vehicle to our showroom for a detailed evaluation.

Can I test drive a vehicle before purchasing?

+

Absolutely! We encourage test drives to ensure you find the perfect vehicle. You can schedule a test drive online, by phone, or by visiting our showroom. Our team will arrange everything for you.

Do you offer warranties on your vehicles?

+

Yes, all our vehicles come with comprehensive warranty options. We offer both manufacturer warranties and extended warranty packages for additional peace of mind. Our team will explain all available options.

What is your delivery process?

+

We offer premium delivery service to your location. Our team will deliver your vehicle, complete all paperwork, and provide a comprehensive orientation of your new car's features. Delivery is available throughout the tri-state area.
